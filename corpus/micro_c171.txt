Helicopter parents are driven by their need to protect and help their children. They want to make sure that their child is safe and be there when their child is in need. Although this behavior may seem to hinder the child's independence and self reliance, the child also has the knowledge that their parent will always be there for them. The child will know that if there is something that he strives to do or comprehend, his helicopter parent will be there to help him out. HELICOPTER PARENTS ARE GOOD FOR THEIR CHILDREN.
