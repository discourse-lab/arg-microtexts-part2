Social Media has improved the lives of teenagers. Social media has allowed teenagers a constant way to communicate with each other as well as an easy way to find new potential friends. Numerous people meet new people everyday using platforms such as Facebook and Twitter from the comfort of their own homes. Social Media also allows teenagers to get a look at someone's life before learning more about them. Some social media platforms even make money for teenagers if they have enough followers. All in all, I would definitely say that social media has improved the life of teenagers.
