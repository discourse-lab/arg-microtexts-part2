There are many benefits to using LED lights. The major one being that they have a longer life than traditional light bulbs, thus making an impact on the environment. They also use less energy to run, conserving energy and cutting costs to consumers. The only drawback is that buying them is more expensive. This initial investment, though, far outweighs the negative effects when you think about the savings you will incur in the future in energy bills and not having replace light bulbs as frequently.
